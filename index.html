/02 ENGINEERING

Every subsystem tested to the point of failure — then spec'd below it.

Thermal, electrical, optical. Three engineering disciplines running in parallel on every fixture we ship. Here's what that actually means on a unit level.

Precision Optics

Tunable beam angles 18° / 30° / 50° in a single housing. UV-stabilized lenses with zero yellowing throughout rated life.

OPTIC / 01

Thermal Architecture

Fin-structured aluminum heat sink with passive airflow channels. Board-level temperature monitoring. Automatic brightness throttling at threshold.

THERMAL / 02

Smart Drivers

DALI-2 and DMX512 configurable. Surge protection to 20 kV. Power factor corrected to 0.95+. Flicker-free below 1% ripple.

ELECTRICAL / 03

L70 Rated Life

80,000 hours lumen maintenance. Backed by a 10-year warranty covering driver failure, lumen depreciation, and ingress defects.

DURATION / 04

IP67 Sealed

Silicone-gasketed hermetic sealing. Submersible to 1 meter. Dust-tight. Rated for operation from -40°C to +55°C ambient.

SEALING / 05

Broadcast Ready

EN 61547 strobe-metric compliant. Verified for HDTV and 4K broadcast at up to 1000 fps without visible banding artifacts.

BROADCAST / 06

Modular Servicing

Field-replaceable drivers, LED boards, and optics. Tool-less access. On-site swap in under 10 minutes, no full unit replacement needed.

SERVICE / 07

Photometric Study

Every project receives a full photometric analysis — pole height, luminaire placement, beam overlap, uniformity target, and spill control — before shipping.
